The pseudo inverse {{< tex >}}A^+ \in \mathbb{R}^{m\times n} {{< /tex >}}, also known as Moore-Penrose inverse, exists for any matrix A and is calculated like this:
{{< tex >}}
A^+ = (A^TA)^{-1}A^T
{{< /tex >}}
